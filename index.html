WHO WE ARE ​
We were founded in Hong Kong (2016) and specialize in wholesale trading of toys, models, gaming software, and accessories chiefly from Japan and Europe. Our main focus business is the distributor of Konami (Yu-Gi-Oh!) in Indonesia. We provide quality service and efficient purchases to our customers.
